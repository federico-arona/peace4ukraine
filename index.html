The Project

The Project

An NFT collection for peace and freedom

The symbol of peace was born in the late 50's from the mind of an English artist, Gerald Holtom, as an emblem of the global battle for Nuclear Disarmament.

Now that the world has tragically plunged back into the nightmare of the nuclear threat due to an unjust and insane war against Ukraine, this symbol once again represents a powerful pacifist and democratic flag for all of us, on the right side of history.

Our first collection as creators was born from this desire for peace and freedom for all the peoples of the earth and responds to the concrete need to provide humanitarian aid to Ukraine that is living its darkest hour.

100% of the primary sale will be donated to the United Help Ukraine Association which works to distribute donations, medical supplies and food to refugees and people on the battlefield.

aPeace4Ukraine: the NFT that is not only a work of art, but the unique and tangible demonstration that you have been there, your help has arrived.
One piece at a time, let's put together the puzzle of peace
